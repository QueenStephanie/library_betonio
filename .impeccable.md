## Design Context

### Users

Library administrators and librarians managing users, roles, catalog health, and operational settings. Primary use is desktop in back-office contexts, with occasional tablet use.

### Brand Personality

Professional, warm, trustworthy.

### Aesthetic Direction

QueenLib light theme with warm cream background, orange accent, and serif headings paired with clean sans-serif body text. Editorial, refined, and calm rather than flashy.

### Design Principles

- Prioritize clarity and task focus for admin workflows.
- Maintain warm, welcoming tones while feeling authoritative.
- Keep hierarchy strong with consistent typography and spacing.
- Use restrained motion and clear interaction feedback.
- Ensure accessibility with strong contrast and readable sizing.
